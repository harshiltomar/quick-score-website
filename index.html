Extension Details

The Quickscore extension provides real-time updates on various sports, including football, basketball, cricket, and more. Stay informed with the latest scores, news articles, and live updates directly from your browser.

With a user-friendly interface, you can customize your news feed to follow your favorite teams and sports. Enjoy a seamless experience with quick access to all the information you need!

Key Features

  • Real-Time Updates: Get live scores and news updates as they happen.
  • Customizable Feed: Tailor your news feed to follow specific teams and sports.
  • Push Notifications: Receive alerts for important matches and news articles.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Navigate easily through the extension with an intuitive design.
  • Multi-Sport Coverage: From football to basketball, stay updated on all your favorite sports.

Why Choose Quickscore?

Quickscore is designed for sports enthusiasts who want to stay connected with their favorite games. Whether you're at home or on the go, our extension ensures you never miss a moment of the action.

Frequently Asked Questions

What sports does Quickscore cover?

Quickscore covers a wide range of sports including football, basketball, cricket, tennis, and many others.

You can enable notifications in your browser settings to receive updates from the Quickscore extension.

Absolutely! You can choose which sports and teams you want to receive notifications for.

News updates are provided in real-time, ensuring you have the most current information available.
